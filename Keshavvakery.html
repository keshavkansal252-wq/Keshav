The Story of Mimmi's Bakery

Founded in 1985 by Mimmi Anderson, our bakery started as a small family kitchen where love was the main ingredient. What began as baking for neighbors and friends quickly grew into a beloved community bakery.

Three generations later, we still use Grandma Mimmi's original recipes, passed down with the same dedication to quality and tradition. Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to ensure everything is fresh for you.

We believe in the magic of handmade goods, the importance of real ingredients, and the power of a warm smile with every purchase. This isn't just a bakery ‚Äì it's where memories are made, one bite at a time.
